**An implant that listens to your body and speaks to you with your doctor's voice before you lose control.**

For people with bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, severe anxiety, OCD, and other psychiatric conditions. Implanted behind the ear, on the bone. Invisible. Comfortable. No battery needed.

## What it does

- **Senses**: Measures your movement, pulse, temperature, and stress level.
- **Detects**: An AI learns your patterns and anticipates a crisis before it explodes.
- **Speaks to you**: Activates your doctor's voice (or the voice you choose) through bone conduction. You hear it inside your head. It gives instructions, calms you, guides you.
- **Alerts (only if necessary)**: If you are at home, data goes to the cloud. An AI filters false alarms. Your doctor only receives an alert if it is real.

## Why it is different

- **No battery needed**: Recharges with your movement and body temperature (bioelectric charging). No replacement needed.
- **You don't have to do anything**: It works automatically, whether you are asleep or awake.
- **It speaks with a voice you trust**: It is not an alarm. It is a doctor who supports you.
- **Works for almost all psychiatric disorders**: Not limited to just one.

## What happens if there is no coverage?

The device still works. It detects the crisis and speaks to you. The cloud is only to alert the doctor.
